Jupiter is the fifth planet from the Sun and the largest in the Solar System. It is a gas giant with a mass more than two and a half times all other planets combined.

Saturn

Saturn is the sixth planet from the Sun and the second-largest in the Solar System, after Jupiter. It is known for its prominent ring system consisting of ice particles and rocky debris.

Diameter 116,460 km
Distance from Sun 1.4 billion km
Day Length 10.7 hours
Moons 82+

Uranus

Uranus is the seventh planet from the Sun. It has the third-largest planetary radius and fourth-largest planetary mass in the Solar System. It is the only planet whose name is derived from a figure from Greek mythology.

Diameter 50,724 km
Distance from Sun 2.9 billion km
Day Length 17.2 hours
Moons 27

Neptune

Neptune is the eighth and farthest-known planet from the Sun. It is the fourth-largest planet by diameter, the third-most-massive planet, and the densest giant planet.

Diameter 49,244 km
Distance from Sun 4.5 billion km
Day Length 16.1 hours
Moons 14

About Cosmic Explorer

Cosmic Explorer is an interactive web experience designed to bring the wonders of our solar system to your screen. Our mission is to inspire curiosity about space and make astronomy accessible to everyone.

This project combines scientific data with beautiful visuals to create an engaging educational tool. Whether you're a student, a teacher, or simply a space enthusiast, we hope Cosmic Explorer sparks your imagination and deepens your appreciation for the cosmos.

All planetary data is sourced from NASA's public databases and is regularly updated to ensure accuracy.
